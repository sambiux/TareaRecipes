BURGER RECIPE


recipe description

Classic Burger: Juicy grilled beef patty with fresh lettuce, tomato, crunchy onions, and melted cheddar cheese, all served on a soft, toasted bun with ketchup and mustard..


INGREDIENTS

  • Hamburger Bun: Typically a soft, round bread, sometimes topped with sesame seeds.

  • Beef Patty:Usually made from 100% ground beef, seasoned with salt and pepper.

  • Cheese:Cheddar is the most common, but you can use others like Swiss, American, or blue cheese.

  • Lettuce:Adds freshness and a bit of crunch

  • Tomato: Sliced, it adds juiciness and flavor.

  • Onion: Can be raw or caramelized, depending on your preference

  • Pickles: Provide a tangy crunch.

  • Sauces: Such as mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, or any other sauces you like.

STEPS TO FOLLOW

  1. Form Patties: Mix the beef with salt and pepper. Shape into 4 patties, making a small dent in the center.

  2. Cook: Cook the patties in a hot pan for 3-4 minutes per side, adding cheese in the last minute if desired.

  3. Toast Buns:Toast the buns in the pan or on a grill.

  4. AssembleSpread sauces on the buns, add the patty, and top with lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles.
