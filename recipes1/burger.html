Strawberry juice recipe


Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries washed and hulled

  • 1 cup water (adjust to desired consistency)

  • Sugar: 2-3 tablesp

  • Lemon juice

STEPS TO FOLLOW

  1. Wash the strawberries:Rinse the strawberries under cold water and remove the stems.

  2. Blend the strawberries:Place the strawberries in a blender. Add the sugar and lemon juice if using. Pour in one cup of water.

  3. Blend until smooth:Blend until the strawberries are fully pureed and the sugar is dissolved.

  4. Strain the juice (optional): For a smoother texture, strain the juice through a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth to remove seeds and pulp.

  5. Adjust the consistency:Add more water if needed to reach your desired consistency.

  6. Serve:Pour the juice into glasses. If you prefer it cold, add ice to taste.
